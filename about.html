About Me

How did I start?

Ever since I was a child I had an interest in cameras, from the toy camera that would change images when you clicked the shutter, to an old poloroid camera my Dad used to own. Some would say it was my calling but for me it was just something I loved to do and I was lucky enough to make it my profession.

How did I start photographing architecture?

I originally started because a good friend of mine wanted me to take some professional pictures of some of his more contemporary projects for a showcase. It was then that I started to appreciate to angles and also the look of some raw materials that are used to construct these buildings, so I decided to take more and more pictures of buidings that peaked my interest and my love for the look of some of these buildings i still as strong as ever!

Why the variety of photography?

I get asked this quite a lot and to answer this in more simple terms I would say doing only one thing in life simply can't be forfilling, at least for me. But I comes down to my drive to capture what I see in my lense and what appeals to my sense of enjoyment. You can say "But architecture and weddings are vastly different" and yes that's true, but in my case I get as much joy snapping the perfect picture of a beautiful building as I do when I see the faces of newly weds when they see the pictures from their special day, so for me I will just keeping doing what makes me happy and I hope my work can maybe inspire others to take up a camera and share what drives them and makes them happy!
